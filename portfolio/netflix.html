Netflix TV Shows & Movies Analysis

With a love for film and streaming shows on Netflix, I decided to practise my data cleaning and visualisation skills on a Netflix TV shows and movies dataset found on Kaggle. Through a step-by-step process, the analysis begins with creating my database and cleaning the dataset through PostgreSQL, followed by visualisations made in Tableau. My complete dashboard can be viewed alongside the files employed at the end of the analysis.
