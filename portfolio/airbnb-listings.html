Airbnb Listings Analysis

This group presentation aimed to conduct an exploratory data analysis of Airbnb's listings dataset downloaded from Maven Insights. The analysis included City Insights, Accommodation Analysis and the Impact of Host Tenure on Airbnb Prices.

Alongside fellow team members, my role was to explore city insights and analyse potential variables that could affect accommodation pricing.
