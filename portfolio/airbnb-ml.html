Unsupervised ML for Airbnb

This group report highlights how using classical unsupervised machine learning can aid Airbnb hosts in knowing how to price their listings accurately. In turn, it optimises the likelihood of receiving bookings, mitigates potential revenue loss due to overpricing, and aids hosts in making data-driven pricing decisions.

Within this report, appropriate data cleaning and feature engineering methods are implemented, our chosen algorithm (K-means clustering) is presented, additional tools and software are recommended, and limitations within machine learning are identified.

Alongside a fellow team member, my main areas of focus were data cleaning, feature engineering, and identifying the main limitations of machine learning.
